About our company

This company started as a single project with the desire to make inspiring games and useful tools for Windows users!

We are a small group of developers based in Germany. For our games we primarily use Game Maker Studio 2.

Faded

Faded is a pixel RPG with an cinematic story.

Explore the world, forge bonds with unforgettable characters, and lose yourself in the original soundtrack as you carve your own path.

Every decision you make shapes the narrative, multiple branching paths lead to entirely different endings, making every playthrough unique.

Faded is currently in early development, and we are actively searching for Beta Testers!
