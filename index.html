Faded

Faded is a pixel RPG game with a deep and mysterious story.

Explore the world and make new friends with all of our characters!
Enjoy the wonderful music while making your own story.

Faded is a game where you create your own story based on the actions you made in game, reaching different endings!

Faded is still in early development and we are currently in search for Beta testers! If interested contact us by our media or by email!
